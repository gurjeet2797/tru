Sigilai

privacy policy

Overview

Sigilai is a multi-perspective thought engine. We believe in transparency about how your data is handled — the short version is: we collect very little and share nothing.

Data We Collect

  • Questions you ask: Your messages are sent to our server to generate responses. We do not permanently store your conversations.
  • No account required: Sigilai does not require sign-up, login, or any personal information.
  • No tracking: We do not use analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking pixels.

Third-Party Services

Sigilai uses OpenAI's API to generate responses. Your questions are sent to OpenAI for processing. OpenAI's data usage policies apply to that processing. We do not send any identifying information — only the text of your question.

For details, see OpenAI's Privacy Policy.

Data Storage

  • Conversations exist only in your device's memory during your session.
  • When you close the app, conversations are not persisted.
  • Our server does not store your messages after generating a response.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to any third parties beyond what is described above (OpenAI for response generation).

Children's Privacy

Sigilai does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is rated 4+ and contains no objectionable content.

Changes

If we update this policy, the revised version will be posted here with an updated date.
